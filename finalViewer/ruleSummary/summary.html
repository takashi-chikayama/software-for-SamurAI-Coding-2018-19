SamurAI Jockey 2018-2019 Rule Summary
  • The game is a two player race.
  • In each step, players can change their speed by ±1, in either x or y direction, or both.
  • There are obstacles and/or puddles in the course.
    • Moves going out of the course or touching an obstacle are canceled.
    • Players' speed becomes zero when their moves end at paddles. Note that moves can jump over paddles.
  • Players have limited fields of vision. Obstacles far away cannot be seen. Two players have the same field of vision; the follower has some advantage.
  • When players' moves cross or touch each other, the move without priority is canceled.
    • The player behind (with the smaller y-axis) has the priority.
    • When at the same y-axis, one to the left (with the smaller x-axis) at start has the priority.
    • Exception: When the move touches the square of the opponent's original position, it is canceled.
  • A match comprises of two races on the same race course, with the start positions exchanged. One with the shorter total goal times is the winner.
